💫 Who I am & What Moves me

Game Developer & Creative Storyteller with a visual heart

I’m Deryana Dhuzent, a Mexican creative technologist based in Vancouver. I studied video game development, but my path has been anything but linear. Through unexpected turns, I’ve built a multidisciplinary practice that blends code, art, and community. What moves me is the power of creativity to heal, connect, and transform. Whether I’m painting, coding, or designing for a small business, I create with intention—always thinking about how it feels, how it functions, and who it serves.

🎨 Where Art Meets Tech

My artistic and technical paths aren’t separate, they’re intertwined. I approach programming like a artist: with curiosity, iteration, and a deep respect for process. And I approach art like a developer: with structure, logic, and a desire to build something meaningful. This fusion shows up in everything I do, from game prototypes to branding projects to community murals. It’s not about choosing one side; it’s about letting both inform each other.

🌱 What Sets Me Apart

-I work iteratively, embracing feedback and evolution. -I bring cultural sensitivity and bilingual fluency to every project. -I prioritize ethics, accessibility, and emotional resonance in my creative decisions. -I thrive in collaborative spaces, especially those rooted in community care. -My portfolio isn’t just a showcase, but a reflection of my values, my growth, and my commitment to creating with purpose.

🧭 My Journey

I studied video game development at Vancouver Film School during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic. While I graduated with technical training, visa delays and personal challenges made it difficult to enter the industry right away. In that uncertain time, art became my refuge, a way to process emotions, reconnect with myself, and begin healing. Somehow, it also became the foundation of my business. What started as therapy evolved into a visual language that now lives across illustrations, paintings, and community projects.

🧠 How I Work

My creative process is iterative, intuitive, and deeply human. I blend technical precision with emotional storytelling, whether I’m coding a game mechanic or designing a visual identity. I care about aesthetics, functionality, and how each piece connects with its audience. My work is shaped by cultural sensitivity, ethical values, and a commitment to community.
